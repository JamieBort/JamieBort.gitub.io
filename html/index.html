A little bit about myself

I use software to solve problems and create things. Through this website I hope to share a bit of my knowledge with others.

I am a member and co-organizer of . is a community of people in technology, primarily but not exclusively software developers. Many have made the switch to the tech field from unrelated careers. Others are recent computer science graduates. And a minority have been in the field for many years. We'll collaborate on projects, discuss the minutia of work life, and once in a while, we'll meet for a social event. Before the pandemic we hosted weekly coding nights; where we'd meet at a coffee shop to collaborate on projects.

Some other things that have my attention at the moment:

  • Data visualization - It's a fun and challenging way to represent the data that I enjoy crunching.
  • Presenting - Frankly the idea of it scares me. But I know it will help me become a better communicator, and by extension, a better coder and teammate.
  • Mentors - I could use one. But I also enjoy mentoring others.

I hope you find this site to be a fun and valuable source for knowledge no matter where you are on your coding journey!
